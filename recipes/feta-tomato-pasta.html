Baked Feta Tomato Pasta

Description:

Give this easy-to-make feta tomato pasta a try - it's full of flavor and made with just a few simple ingredients!

Ingredients:

  • 200g feta cheese
  • 500g cherry tomatoes
  • 1 minced large garlic clove
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 400g dried pasta of choice
  • 3 tbsp dried or chopped fresh basil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C.
  2. Place the feta block in the middle of the baking dish surrounded by the cherry tomatoes, and coat everything with the olive oil.
  3. Season with salt, black pepper, and dried basil (if you're not using fresh basil).
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, until the cherry tomatoes burst, and the feta is soft.
  5. While the tomatoes and feta are baking, cook the pasta in a pot of salted water according to package instructions until al dente.
  6. Remove the baking dish from the oven, add the garlic, and fresh basil leaves (if using), then use a fork to squash the tomatoes, and stir through the baked feta.
  7. Add the pasta to the sauce and toss to combine. Garnish with more fresh basil and serve warm.
