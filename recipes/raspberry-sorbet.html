Raspberry Sorbet

Description:

Try this honey-sweetened raspberry sorbet recipe, offering a sweet-tart taste - no ice cream machine needed!

Ingredients:

  • 400g fresh or frozen raspberries
  • 100g honey
  • 200g water
  • 50g lime juice
  • 1 lime zest

Steps:

  1. Place all ingredients into a blender or food processor and blend together until smooth.
  2. If an extra smooth sorbet is desired, press mixture through a fine sieve to remove seeds.
  3. Pour into a freezer safe container and place into the freezer overnight or for at least 3 hours.

Any leftovers can be stored in a freezer safe airtight container for up to 1 month.
