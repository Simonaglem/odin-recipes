Homemade Lemonade

Description:

This sweet and tart lemonade is easy to make and incredibly refreshing - definitely worth a try!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lemon
  • 25g sugar
  • 400ml water

Steps:

  1. Boil the kettle and peel half the lemon.
  2. Put the peelings of zest in a jug with the sugar.
  3. Pour over 150ml of boiling water and leave the zest to steep the water.
  4. Store in the fridge until cold.
  5. Once chilled, top the lemonade with the remaining 250ml cold water and the juice of the lemon.
  6. Serve the lemonade with lots of ice.
